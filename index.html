Where should I move?

A study on the countries available in WVS database

The mission

I'm conceptually thinking of briefly moving to another country when I graduate. I love Sweden, but you can't live with swedish winter your whole life.
Therefore I would like to evaluate a couple of different living alternatives based on how they compare to Sweden in a couple of values that I value myself! For this I will try the WVS (World Values Surveys) tool for analysing nations.

Analytical Trail of WVS

1. Select wave
WVS wants me to choose "a wave". That is what they call a time period of between 4-5 years. New terminology is thrown at me from the start. I want the newest data, so I click on 2010-2014.

2. Select nations
Here I get a list of available countries. I would like to choose Malta, but it isn't on the list, so I compromise and selects Cyprus instead. Hopefully they are close. Singapore is also interesting, and I'm a little curious of New Zealand. And of course I want to compare it to Sweden.

3. Select question
I would prefer to compare several values at once, but I can only do one at the time it seems.

4. Table of data
A sexy table of data is presented to me. But I'm a modern human being who is easily bored by pure numbers. I want my information wrapped in graphics.

5. A simple diagram
When I look at this plot, I don't know what I'm looking at, and it stresses me. Too many colors. Too much gazing around and remembering colors. I hastens myself in finding a different view of the data.

6. Pie chart
The pie chart. Finally a familiar face! However, I can only look at one nation at a time, and a lot of space is wasted... I would want some kind of tool that shows several nations simultaneously.
